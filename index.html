Introducing

Anoma Commonwealth of Mages — by Wizard

We’re evolving how the Anoma Commonwealth of Mages grows. From here on, advancement won’t happen in isolation – it will come through collaboration. Cohorts of five, led by seasoned mages, building content, games, rituals, and new activations that push the island forward.

Why it matters
Rank-up flow

Create → Ship → Advance

  1. Cohort designs an initiative (content / game / ritual / activation).
  2. Lead mage ships the initiative with the team.
  3. Lead submits contributors → each member ranks up +1.
Leadership pool: @Wizard, @Grand Master, @Intent Master.
Why it matters

From solo spells to collective magic

Deeper collaboration

Shared creation builds stronger bonds and better ideas. We combine skills across design, dev, storytelling, and ops.

Mentorship & growth

Rising mages work directly with veteran role-holders — learning how to plan, ship, and lead.

Clear advancement

Each successful initiative is a visible milestone. "+1 rank" for every contributor on completion.

What’s changing

Small, focused cohorts led by senior mages

Structure

Cohorts of 5 mages. Each cohort is led by a Wizard, Grand Master, or Intent Master.

Output

Ship tangible work: content, mini‑games, community rituals, integrations, or new activations.

Recognition

Lead submits a contributor list after shipping. All contributors are eligible to rank up one level.
